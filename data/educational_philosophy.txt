Gujarat Vidyapith's education system includes: regular participation in community work, residential life, social service, community prayers, simple and self-reliant living, study tours and field studies, hand spinning and craft work training. The mother tongue (Gujarati) is the primary medium of instruction. Hindi-Hindustani teaching is compulsory. Vocational training receives equal importance as intellectual education. The focus is on village uplift and rural education, as national growth depends on villages, not cities. There is complete religious tolerance and spiritual development through knowledge of religion in consonance with truth and non-violence.
