The twelve key principles of Gujarat Vidyapith include: (1) Preparing workers for national regeneration according to Gandhian ideals, (2) Using only means consistent with truth and non-violence, (3) Regarding untouchability as a blot on Hinduism and working for its removal, (4) Regular hand-spinning and wearing khadi as essential for national regeneration, (5) Mother tongue as primary language of instruction, (6) Compulsory Hindi-Hindustani teaching, (7) Equal importance to vocational and intellectual education, (8) Primary focus on village education over cities, (9) Prioritizing needs of village-dwellers, (10) Complete tolerance of all religions, (11) Spiritual development through religious knowledge based on truth and non-violence, (12) Compulsory physical exercise and manual work training.
