Gujarat Vidyapith is a deemed university recognized by UGC since 1963. It offers integrated education from Nursery to Doctorate level including Bachelor's, Master's, M.Phil., and Ph.D. programs in various disciplines. Early subjects included Gujarati, Marathi, Bengali, Sanskrit, Persian, English, History, Mathematics, Philosophy, Economics, Accountancy, Music, Political Science, Pharmacy, Archeology and Indian Studies. The institution has been accredited by NAAC and is recognized in NIRF and AISHE rankings. It continues to promote Gandhian ideals of service-oriented education.
