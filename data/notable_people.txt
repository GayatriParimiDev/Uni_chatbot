Mahatma Gandhi served as Chancellor from 1920 to 1948. After Gandhi, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, Dr. Rajendra Prasad, and Morarji Desai served as Chancellors. Professor A.T. Gidwani was the founder Vice-Chancellor. Other notable figures include Kasturba Gandhi, Mahadev Desai, Narharibhai Parikh, and various other freedom fighters who taught and studied at the institution.
