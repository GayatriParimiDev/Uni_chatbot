Gujarat Vidyapith was established on October 18, 1920. Gujarat Mahavidyalaya was established on November 15, 1920. The first convocation was held on June 12, 1922 under the chairmanship of Vallabhbhai Patel. The Vidyapith temporarily stopped functioning during civil disobedience movements from 1930-1935 and again during the Quit India movement from 1942-1945, as students and faculty actively participated in the freedom struggle. After 1945, activities resumed. In June 1947, Mahadev Desai Samaj Sewa Mahavidyalaya was established.
